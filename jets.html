Stratos 714

"The Stratos 714 is a Very Light Jet (VLJ) designed to meet the needs of a growing number of performance-oriented aviators who demand more speed and more range in an owner-flown aircraft. Flying high above the weather at FL 410, the Stratos 714 can traverse the United States from Miami to Seattle in seven hours flight time against 50 knots headwinds, with a single stop and a full payload. Until now, no personal aircraft could accomplish this feat."
